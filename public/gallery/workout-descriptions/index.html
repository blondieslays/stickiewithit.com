Standing Biceps Curl

Workout: Arms

Muscle Group: Biceps

🏃‍♀️ Get Ready: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, weights in each hand.

💪 Set, Go: Curl them towards your shoulders & hold for a count. Lower slowly beside your hips. Repeat 12 times.

🔥 Hot Tip: Keep your elbows close to your torso as you curl.

Equipment: Weights (2)

Half-Rolled Back Biceps Curl

Workout: Arms

Muscle Group: Biceps

🏃‍♀️ Get Ready: Sit with knees bent, resistance band wrapped around feet. Hold band in fists with thumbs pointing up.

💪 Set, Go: Curl band towards your shoulder, hold, then return to 90°. Repeat 12 times.

🔥 Hot Tip: Keep your lumbar spine in a "C" shape, & shoulders back.

Equipment: Resistance band

Banded Zip-Ups

Workout: Arms

Muscle Group: Biceps

🏃‍♀️ Get Ready: Stand on a resistance band, feet shoulder-width apart, with thumbs up.

💪 Set, Go: Lift band to chest level, hold, then lower. Repeat 12 times.

🔥 Hot Tip: Keep pelvis tucked & slight bend in knees.

Equipment: Resistance band
